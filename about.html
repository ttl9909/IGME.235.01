RUNGUY

A simple platformer, in a hand drawn style.

Genre

Platforming Run and Gun.

Platform

Desktop only.

Story

A storyless, objectiveless, run to the exit to continue game.

Esthetic

Very cartoon-y and simple looking game, all drawn in MSPaint.

Gameplay

Controls

Arrow keys for movement.
Space to shoot.

Onboarding

A brief tutorial to teach the player the controls.

Player Learning

Simple movement should be relativly straight forward to learn, the only other thing is incorporating the shooting mechanic to make running to the exit easier.
